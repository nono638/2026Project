RAGBench

A configurable evaluation pipeline for Retrieval-Augmented Generation

RAGBench runs the full cartesian product of RAG configurations (chunker × embedder × strategy × language model), scores the results with LLM judges, and identifies optimal configurations for different constraints. It answers: when does a small model with a smart strategy outperform a larger model with naive RAG?

Key Findings

From Experiment 0v3 — 500 HotpotQA questions scored by 6 LLM judges (3 Gemini + 3 Claude).

Claude Haiku is the most accurate LLM judge

Highest correlation with gold F1 (r=0.450) at n=500 — and the cheapest Anthropic option at $0.002/call.

Gemini 2.5 Pro is the best free budget scorer

r=0.348 with gold F1 via Google AI Studio — no API cost for evaluation.

Strong inter-judge agreement among top scorers

Sonnet-Opus r=0.820, Flash-Pro r=0.892 — judges broadly agree on answer quality.

76% exact match on HotpotQA — answer quality is solid

Retrieval failures at 14%, generation failures at 10%. Pipeline works well out of the box.
